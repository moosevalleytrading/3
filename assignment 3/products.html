Our Products

The Robo Builder 3000 is an educational robotics kit that teaches children the basics of programming and engineering. With easy-to-connect parts and an intuitive interface, kids can build their own functioning robots while learning STEM concepts.

The Magic Art Coloring Set combines traditional coloring with augmented reality technology. Children can color their drawings and watch them come to life on a tablet or smartphone, encouraging both artistic expression and digital literacy.

AdventureQuest Treasure Hunt is an interactive board game that combines physical play with problem-solving challenges. Players follow clues, solve puzzles, and work together to find hidden treasures, developing critical thinking and cooperation skills.
